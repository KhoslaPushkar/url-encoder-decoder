Discover our mission to revolutionize web development by providing free, enterprise-grade URL encoding tools trusted by developers worldwide since 2024.

Our Story

URL Encoder Decoder was founded in 2024 by a team of experienced software engineers and web development specialists based in Mumbai, India, who recognized the critical need for reliable, secure, and accessible URL encoding solutions. After years of working with Fortune 500 companies and startups alike, we witnessed developers consistently struggling with URL encoding challenges that led to security vulnerabilities, data corruption, and integration failures across web applications.

Our founding team brings over 15 years of combined experience in full-stack development, cybersecurity, and API design. We've worked extensively with technologies including Python, JavaScript, Java, PHP, and modern web frameworks, giving us deep insights into the real-world challenges developers face when handling URL encoding across different platforms and programming languages.

What started as an internal tool for our development projects quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform when we realized the broader developer community needed access to the same reliable, standards-compliant URL encoding solutions we were using. We believe that every developer, regardless of their experience level or budget, deserves access to professional-grade tools that enhance productivity and ensure application security.

Our Mission

At URL Encoder Decoder, our primary mission is to democratize web development by providing enterprise-quality, completely free tools for effective URL manipulation and encoding. We understand that proper URL handling is fundamental to building secure, SEO-friendly, and user-accessible web applications. Our tools are engineered to eliminate complexity while drastically reducing development time, ensuring that developers can focus on building innovative solutions rather than wrestling with encoding standards.

We go beyond simply providing tools by serving as an educational resource for the global developer community. Our comprehensive guides, tutorials, and best-practice documentation help developers understand not just the "how" but the "why" behind proper URL encoding. We cover critical topics including security implications, SEO optimization, internationalization support, and compliance with web standards like RFC 3986.

Looking ahead, we're committed to continuous innovation and community-driven development. We actively incorporate user feedback, stay current with emerging web standards, and regularly expand our platform with new features and educational content. Our vision is to maintain a completely free, open-access environment where developers worldwide can access professional-grade resources that were traditionally available only to large enterprises with substantial tool budgets.

Whether you're a solo developer building your first web application, a startup team creating the next breakthrough platform, or an enterprise development team working on mission-critical systems, URL Encoder Decoder is designed to scale with your needs while maintaining the same level of reliability and performance.
