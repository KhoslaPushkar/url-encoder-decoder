Privacy Policy

Learn how we protect your data and respect your privacy when using our URL encoding and decoding tools.

At URL Encoder Decoder, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and tools. We designed our tools with privacy in mind, ensuring that your data remains secure and under your control.

Information We Collect

We believe in minimizing data collection while providing valuable services. Here's what we collect and why:

Information You Provide

When you use our URL encoding/decoding tools:

  • We process the URLs and text you input for encoding/decoding
  • This processing happens entirely in your browser - we don't send your data to our servers
  • We don't store the content you process in our databases
  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may collect standard web analytics data:

  • Your IP address (anonymized for analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit and time spent on them
  • Referring website addresses
  • Other standard web analytics data that helps us improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our URL encoding/decoding services
  • Understand how visitors use our website to enhance user experience
  • Monitor and prevent technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations when required
  • Analyze trends to improve our tool's functionality and performance

Data Processing and Storage

Our URL encoding/decoding tools work entirely client-side:

  • Your data is processed in your web browser using JavaScript
  • We don't transmit your input data to our servers
  • We don't store the content you process
  • No personal information is required to use our tools

For web analytics, we may use services like Google Analytics that collect standard usage data. This data is aggregated and anonymized, meaning it cannot be traced back to individual users.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide basic functionality like session management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. We don't use cookies to track your browsing activity outside our website.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect limited information:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics (data anonymized)
  • Cloudflare for security and performance
  • Other services that help us operate our website efficiently

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they use information. We carefully select service providers who respect user privacy and adhere to data protection standards.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to collected data
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information using commercially acceptable means, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your information
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to delete your information
  • Right to restrict processing of your information
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@urlencoderdecoder.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
